Fellows — Mentoring & Research
Opportunities within the Division of Cardiovascular Medicine at University of Michigan
🌟
Interested in an opportunity? Contact the Program Coordinator or reach out directly to the faculty PI listed. Additional listings available on the Division Research Portal →
🤝 Mentoring
Career Development Mentorship Program
Coordinator: Fellowship Program Office
One-on-one mentoring pairing with faculty across all subspecialties. Focus on career planning, board prep, and academic development throughout fellowship.
All FellowsCareer
🔬 Research
PREDICT-HF Biomarker Study
PI: Dr. Scott Hummel · Heart Failure Section
Investigating novel biomarkers in HFpEF. Fellow role includes protocol coordination, data analysis, and manuscript preparation. Clinical research exposure.
HFpEFBiomarkersClinical Research
🔬 Research
AI-Assisted Echo Interpretation Project
PI: Dr. Venkatesh Murthy · Imaging Section
Machine learning models applied to echo reads at scale. Fellows participate in dataset curation, model evaluation, and co-authorship on publications.
ImagingAI/MLEcho
🤝 Mentoring
Women in Cardiology Mentorship Circle
Lead: Dr. Kim Eagle · Division Chief Emeritus
Structured peer and faculty mentoring for women in cardiology training. Includes quarterly dinners, speaker series, and networking with alumni.
Diversity & InclusionNetworking
🔬 Research
LVAD Outcomes Registry Analysis
PI: Dr. Francis Pagani · Cardiac Surgery / CV Medicine
Longitudinal outcomes data from Michigan's LVAD program — one of the largest in the country. Fellow opportunity in database analysis and clinical outcomes research.
LVADHFOutcomes Research
🤝 Mentoring
Interventional Cardiology Interest Track
Lead: Interventional Faculty Group
Designed for fellows pursuing interventional fellowship. Structured exposure to complex PCI, structural, and peripheral cases with direct faculty mentoring and application coaching.
